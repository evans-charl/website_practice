Multitier Architecture

The "Too Many Cooks" (but in a good way) strategy.

Instead of one giant computer doing everything, Multitier Architecture breaks a website into layers. One layer handles what you see (UI), one handles the logic (Brain), and one handles the data (Memory). It's like a restaurant: the waiter, the chef, and the dishwasher all have their own jobs so things don't get messy.
