Extranet

The "Guest Pass" network.

An Extranet is an Intranet with a VIP entrance. It allows a company to share specific parts of its internal network with trusted outsiders, like partners or suppliers. It's like letting the pizza delivery guy into the lobby of your apartment building, but not letting him into your actual living room.
