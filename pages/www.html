World Wide Web

The internet's front door.

While the internet is the "pipes," the World Wide Web is the water flowing through them. It's a massive collection of pages linked by clickable text (hyperlinks). Without the Web, the internet would just be a dark room full of blinking servers; the Web turned it into a digital shopping mall, library, and movie theater all at once.
