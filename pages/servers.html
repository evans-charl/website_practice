Web Servers

The librarian who never sleeps.

A Web Server is a high-powered computer that sits in a cold room waiting for someone to ask for a file. When you click a link, the server wakes up, finds the page you want, and throws it across the internet to your browser. It's basically a 24/7 digital vending machine.
