HTML Terminology

The Digital Skeleton.

HTML is the structure of every page you see. It uses "tags" to tell the browser "This is a heading," "This is a paragraph," and "This is a picture of my dog." Without HTML, the web would just be a giant wall of unformatted text. It's the bones that hold the digital body together.
